INTERNAL AUDIT REPORT — CONFIDENTIAL
Audit Cycle: Q1 2026
Subject: Review of DTI Policy Exception Approvals (Home Loans)

1. Scope
This audit reviewed all DTI policy exceptions approved above the 50%
regulatory ceiling during the review period, per RBI/RC-2026/014 Section 3
reporting requirements.

2. Findings
2.1 Of 14 exceptions approved during the period, 12 were correctly
    documented with reviewing-officer rationale on file. 2 exceptions
    (Loan refs LN-2143, LN-2298) were approved but the rationale
    documentation was filed more than 30 days after approval, in
    violation of internal SLA (though within the regulatory 7-year
    retention requirement).

2.2 One case (Loan ref: LN-2276, DTI 54%) was identified where the
    exception was approved by a Senior Underwriter rather than an officer
    at Head of Credit Risk level or above, in violation of Section 3(a) of
    the governing circular. This has been flagged as a control gap and
    referred to Compliance for corrective action.

3. Recommendation
The audit recommends implementing a system-level hard block preventing any
DTI exception above 50% from being finalized without a recorded
Head-of-Credit-Risk-level approval, rather than relying solely on manual
process adherence. This corresponds to the human-in-the-loop escalation
trigger already configured in the underwriting assistant for DTI risk
level HIGH.

4. Status
Corrective action on the control gap (2.2) is in progress; target closure
date is end of Q2 2026. This report and underlying case files are subject
to independent auditor review and are not for general underwriting staff
distribution.
